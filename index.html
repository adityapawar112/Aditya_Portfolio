About

I'am Aditya Pawar

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Aditya Pawar, a passionate and creative technologist with a rich blend of skills in 3D design, full-stack development, and machine learning. With a Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology and Data Science from Ajeenkya D Y Patil University, I have honed my technical prowess and innovative thinking through both academic and professional experiences.
During my tenure as a 3D Designer at Emanate Agency, I crafted high-quality 3D models, animations, and visual effects that significantly enhanced brand storytelling and engagement. My role involved regular client consultations to ensure that project objectives were met and exceeded, refining designs based on client feedback to deliver outstanding results.
I have successfully developed and deployed a social media platform demo, Socialpidia, using Python and Django. This project showcases my full-stack capabilities, demonstrating robust backend architectures and dynamic frontend interfaces. Additionally, I have created machine learning models for diabetes prediction and plant disease classification, utilizing Jupyter Notebook for comprehensive data analysis and visualization.
My technical toolkit includes proficiency in languages such as Java, Python, C, C++, and HTML/CSS, along with expertise in MySQL, Django, and Blender for 3D design. I am well-versed in tools like Visual Studio, Arduino, and GitHub, and have a strong foundation in web development, machine learning, backend development, and 3D design.
Beyond my professional endeavors, I am an active Google Maps contributor and a dedicated member of the Google Developers Club (GDSC). I also manage an Instagram photography account, showcasing my creative photography skills and engaging with a diverse audience. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I am excited to share my journey, skills, and projects with you. Feel free to explore my work and connect with me on LinkedIn and GitHub.
